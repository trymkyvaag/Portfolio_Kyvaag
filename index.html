My Work

Ongoing research: SO(3) equivariant predictions

Researching non-cooperative spacecraft pose estimation, focusing on predicting 6D pose (3D position and rotation) without prior knowledge of spacecraft structure. Exploring SO(3)-equivariant methods and infrared map augmentation to improve symmetry preservation and performance on limited datasets, while evaluating model robustness across varying training data volumes.

  • Representation theory
  • Equivariant networks
  • Pose/Translation estimation
  • Geometric Deep Learning

RL for 2v2 soccer

Optuna training a reinforcement learning agent to play 2v2 soccer with a multi-agent setup. The agent is trained to play against a bot that is trained for defense, while the attacking agents are trained to play offense with realistic soccer rules and tactics.
